About Me

I am an aspiring Data Scientist with a Masters' from NJIT
Acitvely seeking full-time opportunities in Data Science roles.

Me

Though I have spent most of my life in the United States, I still believe my immigrant roots are key to my identity. I was born in India, and I moved to the US when I was 9 years old back in 2005. Fast forward to nine years later, in 2014 I became the second person in my family to attend college—following my sister.

I did my Bachelors’ in Biomedical Engineering from Rutgers University, where I graduated from in 2018 with a Magna Cum Laude distinction. I had initially aspired to attend medical school, but somewhere during my undergraduate program, I developed a passion for data and programming. This passion was rooted in my engineering coursework, along with the academic projects I had the chance to work on with a few of my BME professors.

Following my completion of my undergraduate degree, I worked for a small biotech company as a Product Development Scientist. Having decided against pursuing Medical School, I started developing my programming and data-science skills through projects and online classes—such as Machine Learning A-Z on Udemy, and the famed FastAi course. In January 2019, I enrolled into the Masters’ in Data Science program at NJIT.

This portfolio is a collection of my work, both in and out of school, through which I aim to showcase my skills and abilities.
